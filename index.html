RentLens

Interactive map for rental pricing insights

I created RentLens because I struggled to find reliable rent prices in different neighborhoods. RentLens is a user-friendly tool that shows rent per square foot for various listings on an interactive map.

You can easily filter results in real-time, view heat maps to see price trends, adjust range sliders to fit your budget, and save your favorite listings for easy comparisons.

Currently available for the San Francisco Bay Area, with more cities coming soon. It made house hunting much simpler and transparent for me, and I hope it helps others too!

Pathos

LLM powered news sentiment engine

Pathos is an LLM powered news sentiment engine that curates articles based on your emotional preferences.

I noticed that news today heavily centers around negativity, with a goal to increase engagement through shock value. I created this project to try and reclaim control over our emotional well-being, in our own small way.

The project aims to transform news consumption from a source of stress into a personalized journey that aligns with your feelings. Try it out!
